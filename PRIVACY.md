# Privacy Policy — FindX

**Last updated:** April 3, 2026

## Data Collection

FindX does **not** collect, store, or transmit any user data. The extension runs entirely locally in your browser.

## Permissions

- **activeTab** — Used to access the current tab's page content for searching and highlighting text. Only activates when you click the extension icon or use the keyboard shortcut.
- **scripting** — Used to inject the search overlay and highlighting logic into the current webpage.

## Third-Party Services

FindX does not communicate with any external servers, APIs, or third-party services.
